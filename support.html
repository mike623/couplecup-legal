Common Questions

How do I pair with my partner?

After signing up, tap the pairing icon and share your invite link or code with your partner. Once they accept, your journals are linked.

My photos aren't uploading

Check your internet connection and ensure CoupleCup has camera/photo library permissions in iOS Settings → CoupleCup.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at namike623@gmail.com with the subject "Delete Account" and we'll remove all your data within 30 days.
