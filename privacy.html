Privacy Policy

CoupleCup · Last updated: May 2026

CoupleCup ("we", "our", "us") is a shared memory journal app for couples. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Account data — email address used to create your account.
  • Journal entries — text, dates, moods, and photos you and your partner add.
  • Photos — images you upload are stored securely in our cloud storage.
  • Pairing data — the relationship link between you and your partner's account.

How We Use It

  • To sync your shared journal between you and your partner.
  • To provide authentication and account management.
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at namike623@gmail.com. We will process requests within 30 days.

Children

CoupleCup is not intended for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
